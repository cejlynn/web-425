Stephen King

Stephen King was born in Portland, Mine in 1947. He is the second son of Donald and Nellie Ruth Pillsburry King. He attended grammar school in Durham and then Lisbon Falls High school where he graduated in 1966. He attended the Unioversity of Maine at Orono, and graduated in 1970 with a B.A in English. He married Tabitha Spruce in January of 1971 and made his first professional story sale ("The Glass Floor") to Startling Mystery Stories in 1967. Steph would write in the evenings and on the weekends to produce short stories and work on his novels. In 1973, his novel Carrie got accepted for publication. The sells of this novel allowed him to pursue writing fulltime. Since then Stephen has written several best-selling novels and some that have even turned into tv adaptions and movies.
